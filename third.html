Trading Card Games & money

There many people who live off Trading Card Games that includes many of the players. The main objective why they sell cards is to minimize losses.

Rare cards can sell for less than 1 Dollar or up to thousands and even millions of Dollars depending on their rarity, age and primarily their condition.

The most expensive sold TCG card is the "Honus Wagner 1909-11 T206" which was sold for 3.2 million Dollars

It also set a new world record price for baseball cards. The current most valuable TCG card is the "Tournament Black Luster Soldier"

It was the "Yu-Gi-Oh!" card given for winning the first Yu-Gi-Oh! tournament. It is valued at 10 million Dollars but was sold for 2 million

The card is completely made out of stainless steel and therefor the only one of its kind.

The "Yu-Gi-Oh!" franchise is one of the biggest TCG franchises and they make over 20 billion revenue.

"Magic: The Gathering" made in 2019 over 500 million

Baseball Trading Card Company make over 300 million yearly.

One third of Nintendo's income is made by their Pokemon Trading Card Game and earns them 750 million a year.

There are different prices for buying Booster Packs and that essentially also reflects on the sale price for the cards.
