What is even a Trading Card Game and what does it involve?

In a TCG you fight turn based with your deck against your opponent.

In majority of the TCGs you defeat your opponent by depleting his Life Point also called LP for short.

A Trading card game also called TCG for short is a collectible card game. Mostly involving the genre fantasy or sports.

The different brands of TCGs come with various rules and card types, which are usually quite specific

Often the cards are divided in the three types : offense, defense and support.

To play TCGs analogue in you have to buy and collect cards in order to build a deck and challenge your friends.

You can get cards them from Booster packs, which is a pack full of random cards with different rarities

Usually you buy them at general stores as well as online.

You should buy Display Cases which are several Booster Packs in one case and you can get a discount

Having more cards is better for building your Deck/s to your liking

Some people dont even play the game and just collect cards and sell them
