Trading Card Games

How did Trading Card Games come along, and what what about their popularity?

As you should already know if you've spent some time on this website, the phenomenon of TCGs started with the Release of

of "Magic: The Gathering" in 1993. As it is unclear to say how many people started playing right as it came out it is

however safe to say that by the year 1997 there were approximatly 5 million players around the world. As the years passed it

seemed as though player numbers would increase at a rate of about 1 million new people per year. But this developement came

to a stop in 2001 which is most likely to be the result of the Pokemon TCG which was released 2 Years prior and started

picking up steam in the world of TCGs. The following year yet another new TCG was released in the U.S.A by the name of Yu-Gi-Oh!.

Unlike other TCGs Yu-Gi-Oh! had already established a fanbase in Japan.

In 2020 probably due to a popular Youtuber named Logan Paul more attention is being casted towards the Pokemon TCG. But the reason

for that seems to be because of nothing more than monetary gain when getting really rare cards.
